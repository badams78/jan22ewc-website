Description

This subcluster examines comparative economic performance across regions, with particular focus on US-Europe productivity differentials and structural competitiveness factors. Articles analyze productivity growth rates, regulatory burdens, technological innovation gaps, venture capital flows, and currency impacts on economic performance. Common data sources include McKinsey research, central bank analyses, and financial sector reports comparing metrics like R&D spending, unicorn creation rates, and labor productivity trends. The subcluster emphasizes quantitative benchmarking between major economies, highlighting sectoral differences in innovation capacity and regulatory efficiency that drive divergent growth trajectories.
